Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment card details or bank credentials of any kind.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard measures — including encryption at rest and in transit — to protect your information. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us, and we will action this within a reasonable timeframe.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for infrastructure and analytics (such as hosting providers). These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses session cookies solely to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or share cookie data with advertisers. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect your ability to use the Service.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also object to or restrict certain types of processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided in the app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 5 business days.
